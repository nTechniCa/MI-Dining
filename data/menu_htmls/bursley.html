Bursley

  • 1931 Duffield Street, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109
  • 734-763-1120
  • [email protected]

Think of Bursley as 8 mini-restaurants: Signature for chef’s specials, 24 Carrots for vegan/vegetarian options, Halal for composed Halal entrees, Pizziti for pizza, Wild Fire for grilled favorites, Two Oceans for stir fry, Deli for made to order sandwiches, and Finale for desserts!

To learn more about Michigan Dining’s Halal, Kosher, and Ramadan options click here.

Menu offerings are subject to change; please check menu signage in the dining hall for the most up-to-date information.
