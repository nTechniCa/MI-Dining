South Quad

  • 600 East Madison, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109
  • 734-764-0169
  • [email protected]

Enjoy a tapas-style meal at South Quad, where ten mini restaurants are serving up a wide variety of cuisines. Make your own burrito bowl at Sabroso, grab some stir-fry at Two Oceans, or pick up some Mediterranean from Olive Branch — this is just a small fraction of what’s available. Look out for the kosher and halal dining stations as well!

To learn more about Michigan Dining’s Halal, Kosher, and Ramadan options click here.

Menu offerings are subject to change; please check menu signage in the dining hall for the most up-to-date information.

Kosher Kitchen Hours of Operation:

Sunday-Thursday
Lunch: 11am-2pm
Dinner: 4:30pm-8pm

Friday
Lunch: 11am-2pm
Dinner: Closed

Saturday
Closed

The Kosher kitchen will close at 2:00 pm on Fridays to allow our Mashgiach adequate commuting time before the candle lighting for Shabbat. If you have any concerns please email the South Quad team.
